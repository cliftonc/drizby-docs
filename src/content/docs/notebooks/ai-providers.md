---
title: AI Providers
description: Configure AI providers for notebooks and cube generation
---

Drizby supports multiple AI providers. Configure them from **Settings → AI Provider** in the UI.

| Provider | Package | Models |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Anthropic | `@anthropic-ai/sdk` | Claude 4, Claude 3.5 Sonnet |
| OpenAI | `openai` | GPT-4o, GPT-4 |
| Google | `@google/generative-ai` | Gemini Pro |

Enter your API key for your chosen provider. The key is stored encrypted in Drizby's internal database.
