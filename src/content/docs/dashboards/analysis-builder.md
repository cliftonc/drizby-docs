The analysis builder lets you explore your data visually without writing code. Select metrics (measures), breakdowns (dimensions), and filters — results appear instantly as you build.

## Features

- **Drag-and-drop** query builder with metrics, dimensions, and filters
- **Chart and Table** toggle to switch between visualizations and raw data
- **AI analysis** — click "Analyse with AI" to get AI-generated insights from your query results
- **Share** results directly to dashboards as portlets
- **Query view** — inspect the generated cube query behind any visualization
